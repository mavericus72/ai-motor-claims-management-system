# Domain Model

## User

Represents customer, surveyor or admin.

## Policy

Motor Insurance policy associated with a vehicle.

## Vehicle

Insured vehicle details.

## Claim

Motor Insurance claim raised against a policy.

## ClaimImage

Vehicle damage images uploaded by customer.

## ClaimDocument

Documents uploaded during claim process.

## DamageAssessment

AI driven damage report.

## CostEstimate

Estimated repair cost for the vehicle.

## Settlement

Final claim settlement.

## AuditLog

Tracks every action in system.
