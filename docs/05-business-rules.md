# Business Rules

## Policy Rules

- Policy must be active.
- Policy must not be expired.

## Claim Rules

- Claim must belong to valid policy.
- Claim must have at least one image.
- Driving License should be valid and effective for the class of the vehicle.
- RC must be in the name of the policy holder.

## Document Rules

Own Damage Claim:

- RC
- Driving License

Third Party Claim:

- RC
- Driving License
- FIR

## Settlement Rules

Settlement requires:

- Damage Assessment
- Repair Estimate
- Surveyor Approval

## Audit Rules

All actions must be logged.
